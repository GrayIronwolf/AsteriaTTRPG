# Iron Ore

A common iron-bearing ore used as the foundation for basic smithing and metalworking.

## Effect

Iron Ore can be refined into Iron Ingots using a standard forge.

## Crafting Use

Used for weapons, armour, tools, fittings, and simple metal goods.
