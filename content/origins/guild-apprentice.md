# Guild Apprentice

## Overview
Guild Apprentices were raised or trained around a trade hall, workshop, archive, forge, or professional order.

## Background
They understand hierarchy, contracts, tools, and the value of reputation.

## Story Hooks
- Unfinished apprenticeship
- Guild rival
- Master's missing commission
