# Outcast

## Overview
Outcasts live with distance between themselves and the society that shaped them.

## Background
They may be exiled, blamed, self-banished, or simply unable to belong where they began.

## Story Hooks
- False accusation
- Hidden name
- Settlement that will not forgive
