# Arcana

## Overview
Arcana covers magical theory, spell structures, ritual patterns, and unusual mana phenomena.

## Checks
Use Arcana to identify spells, interpret magical markings, and understand arcane effects.

## Training
Arcana begins at Novice unless modified by race, class, origin, or campaign training.
