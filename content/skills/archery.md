# Archery

## Overview
Archery covers bows, ranged posture, aim, draw control, and battlefield positioning.

## Checks
Use Archery for ranged attacks, difficult shots, moving targets, and precision volleys.

## Training
Archery begins at Novice unless modified by race, class, origin, or campaign training.
