# Stealth

## Overview
Stealth covers quiet movement, hiding, concealment, and avoiding notice.

## Checks
Use Stealth to move unseen, hide in cover, trail a target, or bypass guarded areas.

## Training
Stealth begins at Novice unless modified by race, class, origin, or campaign training.
