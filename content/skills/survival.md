# Survival

## Overview
Survival covers tracking, foraging, shelter, route-finding, and enduring harsh regions.

## Checks
Use Survival for wilderness travel, finding food, reading tracks, and safe camp setup.

## Training
Survival begins at Novice unless modified by race, class, origin, or campaign training.
